================================

1. Double-click TEST_ALL_CAPABILITIES.cmd first.
2. Wait for the green PASS message.
3. Double-click RUN_ME_LOCAL_CLOUDFLARE_READY.cmd to view the protected Professional build.
4. Double-click RUN_ME_LOCAL_EDITION_PREVIEWS.cmd to build and view all three independent editions.
5. Open:

   Edition preview server: http://localhost:8790/community/
   Edition preview server: http://localhost:8790/professional/
   Edition preview server: http://localhost:8790/consultant/

Nothing is deployed by either local runner.
Use RUN_ME_DEPLOY_GIT_AND_CLOUDFLARE_DIRECT.cmd only when a deployment is deliberately required.
