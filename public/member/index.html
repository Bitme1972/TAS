Licence lifecycle

Purchase creates an edition entitlement and update period.
TAS creates a machine-bound activation request without customer evidence.
A private signing service returns the signed .taslic response.
Licence validation unlocks the correct protected download and edition.
Update expiry stops new versions, not the customer’s purchased installed version.
Controlled reset handles legitimate device replacement without enabling reuse abuse.
