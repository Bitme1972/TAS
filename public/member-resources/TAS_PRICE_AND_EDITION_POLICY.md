# TAS launch pricing and edition policy

## TAS Interactive Demo

Free. No account required. Synthetic data only. Non-commercial demonstration.

## TAS Professional

Launch price: **£349**.

Includes perpetual use of the purchased version, one named user, activation on up to three devices and 12 months of updates. Optional updates after the first year: **£129 per year**.

## TAS Consultant

**£899 per year** for one named consultant.

Includes commercial client-assessment rights, consultant identity, engagement references, remediation packs, priority updates and controlled licence resets.

## Future Team and Enterprise

Quotation based. These editions should be introduced only when customer demand justifies multi-user administration, central entitlement management and organisation-specific baselines.

Prices are launch positioning and should be reviewed after the first ten paying customers.
