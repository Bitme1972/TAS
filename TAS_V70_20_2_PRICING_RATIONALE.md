# TAS v70.20.2 Launch Pricing Rationale

Prepared 11 July 2026.

## Recommended launch prices

- TAS Interactive Demo: £0, no account
- TAS Professional: £349 launch purchase
- Professional update entitlement after the included 12 months: £129 per year
- TAS Consultant: £899 per named consultant per year
- Team and Enterprise: quotation only

## Why this position is credible

TAS is a specialist Windows audit-evidence assurance product rather than a broad vulnerability scanner, SIEM or continuous Active Directory monitoring platform. It should therefore launch below large established platforms while charging materially more than a lightweight utility.

Current official reference points reviewed for this decision include:

- ManageEngine ADAudit Plus: annual pricing starts at US$595 for Standard and US$945 for Professional.
- CIS SecureSuite Controls Membership: US$3,600 per year; other membership categories depend on organisation type and permitted use.
- Tenable Nessus Professional: US$4,790 per year.
- Tenable Nessus Expert: US$6,790 per year.
- Netwrix Auditor Essentials: self-service pricing starts at approximately US$20 per enabled identity.

The £349 Professional launch price deliberately reduces first-purchase friction for an unknown new brand while preserving room for a later standard price after customer proof. The £899 Consultant price recognises that one paid client assessment can recover the licence cost and that commercial-use rights must cost more than internal-use rights.

## Commercial rules

Professional is a perpetual licence for the purchased version with 12 months of updates. Update expiry must not disable the purchased version.

Consultant is an annual commercial-use entitlement. It includes client-assessment rights only while the Consultant entitlement is active.

The free Demo must remain synthetic, non-commercial and isolated from the commercial Golden baseline. It demonstrates interaction and reporting, not a recoverable premium dataset.

Prices should be reviewed after the first 20 paid customers, six months after launch or when support demand is measurable, whichever happens first.
