# TAS Guided Product Tour — Narration and Click Script

## Scene 1 — Member Gateway

**Subtitle:** Welcome to Telemetry Assurance Studio. The Member Gateway separates the free synthetic Demo from licensed Professional and Consultant use.

Action: Show the edition cards, pricing, trust position, installer vault and guided tour area.

## Scene 2 — Free Interactive Demo

**Subtitle:** The free Demo uses fixed synthetic evidence and a separate synthetic control pack. It demonstrates the complete interaction pattern without exposing the commercial Golden baseline.

Action: Launch the Demo and show the AURORA dashboard.

## Scene 3 — Evidence Intake

**Subtitle:** Evidence Intake recognises assets, operating-system context and audit policy rows before assurance is generated.

Action: Open Evidence Intake and review the preloaded synthetic evidence.

## Scene 4 — Assurance Results

**Subtitle:** Advanced Plus creates role-aware findings, a Golden comparison and management-ready posture information.

Action: Generate Advanced Plus assurance and open the raw results grid.

## Scene 5 — Row-to-detail interaction

**Subtitle:** Select any result row. The lower pane immediately populates with status, expected and actual settings, event intelligence, frameworks and remediation guidance.

Action: Click a findings row and scroll the lower details pane.

## Scene 6 — Golden comparison

**Subtitle:** The Premium Golden matrix compares multiple assets left to right against one expected audit configuration. The Demo uses synthetic expectations only.

Action: Switch to the Golden matrix, click a server cell and horizontally scroll the matrix.

## Scene 7 — Resizable panes

**Subtitle:** The top results and lower detail areas scroll independently, and the divider can be resized for the task at hand.

Action: Drag the splitter and demonstrate independent scrolling.

## Scene 8 — Before and After

**Subtitle:** Before and After comparison proves remediation by showing improved, regressed and unchanged controls.

Action: Open Before / After and review the fixed synthetic pair.

## Scene 9 — Baseline and Event intelligence

**Subtitle:** The baseline library and Event ID centre connect audit configuration to analyst value, collection sources and detection use cases.

Action: Open Baseline Library, then Event IDs.

## Scene 10 — Reporting

**Subtitle:** TAS produces detailed HTML, CSV, Word and printable report paths, plus Golden comparison and remediation packs.

Action: Return to Assurance Results, export a detailed report and open the output vault.

## Scene 11 — Commercial delivery

**Subtitle:** Licensed customers download the Windows installer from the entitled vault, verify its hash, install in demo mode and activate with a signed machine-bound licence.

Action: Return to the Member Download Vault and show the MSI, hashes and activation steps.
